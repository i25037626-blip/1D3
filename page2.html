🍜 Local Food

Anhui Ban Mian

A famous noodle dish known for its rich broth and handmade noodles. It is a must-try comfort food in Anhui.

Stinky Tofu

Despite its strong smell, this crispy tofu is incredibly delicious and loved by locals.

🏞 Scenic Spots

Huangshan (Yellow Mountain)

One of China's most famous mountains, known for its dramatic peaks, sea of clouds, and ancient pine trees.

Hongcun Village

A beautiful ancient village with traditional architecture, often called a living Chinese painting.
