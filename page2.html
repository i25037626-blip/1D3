About

Anhui is located in eastern China with rich natural scenery.

It has a long history and beautiful countryside views.

Food

Hui cuisine is one of the eight famous Chinese cuisines.

Local dishes are fresh, simple, and full of flavor.

Culture

Huangmei Opera is a famous traditional opera.

Traditional villages show ancient Chinese architecture.

Places

Mount Huangshan is famous for its stunning views.

Hongcun village is a UNESCO World Heritage site.
